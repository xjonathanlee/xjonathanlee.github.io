I am an interdisciplinary designer and maker looking for ways to visualize and harness the power of narrative data. I specialize in media production, user experience and data analysis, on the side I am a photographer and card maker.

In the past I have researched generative design, co-fabrication, and 'contested memory' in architecture and products. As a designer my practice is to make things that tell stories. I believe in equitable design for all, and practice that design "for good" is accessable & fair.
