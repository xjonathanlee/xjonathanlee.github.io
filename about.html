Hi! I’m Jonathan, a photographer + designer based in Vancouver, Canada.

I am a photographer, designer and aspiring architect from Simon Fraser University’s SIAT program. I am a builder and thrill seeker who enjoys conceptualizing new idea’s and playing with all types of materials.

As a designer I firmly believe that good design is useful and functional, but is also inviting and malleable. I am passionate about conceptualizing and articulating ideas in new and exciting ways. I love studying spaces and the things that inhabit them, people in their environments and the curious quirks of how people evolve with their cities.
