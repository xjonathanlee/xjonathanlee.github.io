I’m Jonathan Lee, a photographer + designer based in Vancouver, Canada.

I am an interdisciplinary designer and maker looking for ways to visualize and harness the power of narrative data. I am a recent graduate from the IAT program at Simon Fraser University.

I am interested in citizen science, city space + rewilding projects and photography. In the past I have researched co-fabrication, bio-intelligence, and 'memory-making' in architecture. As a designer my practice is to make things that tell stories beautifully. I believe in equitable design for all, and carry that design "for good" is accessable, fair and follows best practice.

When I am not contending with spreadsheets and design software I like to take pictures of beavers + birds at the pond near my house. On the side I am a professional being awful at DOTA2.
