I am an interdisciplinary designer, and maker studying digital + speculative design research at SFU. I previously recieved a diploma in digital design development at BCIT.

In my current practice I question how we move through the world and how objects in our surroundings dictate how we work + play. Currently I am researching memories in architecture and how we honor thoes that came before us in historic spaces. I am interested in space/place making, digital design and the agency we have over our immediately environment.
