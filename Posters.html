Posters

An Eclectic collection of things I've designed for friends, for projects and for thoes I love. My designs draw inspiration from vintage movie posters. The “end of type” era and bold dynamism by studios like JETSET EXPERIMENTAL, TOMATO and STUDIO GANG. I believe posters are a medium that can convey more than just an idea, but an emotion or a state of mind.

  • Roles: Art Direction, Visual Design
  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Blender, Lightroom
  • Context: Academic + Personal Project
  • Team: Jonathan Lee
